Our Story

Awake&bake was founded by family members who have had 15 years of combined experience in patisserie. We use as many natural products as possible to make our pastries with locally sourced ingredients. We are inspired from international cuisines and aim do deliver a multicultural service. Our products are all available to purchase whether if is for small parties, conferences or events. The aim of awake&bake is to provide an excellent service, with quality goods focusing on customer satisfaction. Whether you have food allergies, preferences, or dietary prescriptions, we always aim to meet your requirements. Based on your experience, we are always open for feedbacks, in order to improve.
